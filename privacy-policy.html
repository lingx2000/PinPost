Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Non-Affiliation Disclaimer: PinPost is an independent personal tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest, Inc. Pinterest® is a registered trademark of Pinterest, Inc.

1. About PinPost

PinPost is a personal desktop scheduling tool that uses the Pinterest API to automate posting of pins to Pinterest boards on your behalf at times you configure. The application runs entirely on your own computer (localhost) and does not operate any public-facing servers.

2. Pinterest API Usage

This application accesses the Pinterest API to:

  • Authenticate your Pinterest account via Pinterest's OAuth 2.0 flow
  • Read your Pinterest boards (to select the correct board for each pin)
  • Create pins on your behalf at scheduled times

This application is not endorsed by or affiliated with Pinterest, Inc. in any way.

3. Data We Collect and Store

All data is stored locally on your device only. We collect:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens — stored encrypted in a local SQLite database on your computer
  • Pinterest username — stored for display purposes only
  • Board names and IDs — cached locally to avoid repeated API calls
  • Pin content you provide — titles, descriptions, image paths/URLs, and destination links that you supply for scheduling

No data is transmitted to any third-party server, cloud service, or analytics platform. All Pinterest API communication is between your computer and Pinterest's servers directly.

4. How Your Data Is Used

Data collected is used solely to post pins to your Pinterest boards on your behalf at the times you configure. It is not used for any other purpose.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

When you disconnect a Pinterest account via the app's Accounts screen, all OAuth tokens, board cache data, and associated pin data for that account are permanently deleted from your local database immediately.

You may also request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below, or by deleting the application's database file (instance/pinpost.db) from your computer.

6. No Reselling or Redistribution

Pinterest data obtained via the Pinterest API — including tokens, board names, pin data, and any other Pinterest-derived information — is not sold, resold, licensed, sublicensed, or redistributed to any third party in any form, under any circumstances.

7. Data Security

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored encrypted using industry-standard symmetric encryption (Fernet / AES-128-CBC) in a local SQLite database. The encryption key is stored only on your device.

8. Third-Party Services

This application uses the Pinterest API, which is subject to Pinterest's own Privacy Policy and Developer Terms of Service.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact
