About PinPost

PinPost is a personal desktop scheduling tool built for creators and small business owners who want to maintain a consistent Pinterest posting schedule without manual effort. The app runs entirely on your own computer — no subscription, no cloud storage, no third-party servers.

It uses the official Pinterest API to authenticate your account and create pins on your behalf. All data stays local to your device.
